Mii Messenger App

Role:

  • UI design
  • Animation

Duration:

April 2021

Tools:

Adobe XD, Photoshop

Overview

Mii is a messenger app I created where I got to practice creating app animation using Adobe XD auto-animate feature. Coincidence or not, I was talking to a friend at that time about our frustrations with a new messenger app we were trying out. When the idea of this project came around, I was eager to design a whole different UI, a much simpler, energetic, vibrant design that solves the complaints we had.
