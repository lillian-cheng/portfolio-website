Cook It! Recipe Responsive Web App

Role:

UI designer, UX designer

  • Wireframing
  • Prototyping
  • UX Research

Duration:

Nov 2020-Feb 2021

Tools:

Adobe XD, Photoshop, InVision, Pen & Paper

Overview

Under the impact of a global pandemic, more people are cooking at home than ever. There's a great need for a well-designed, easy to use, and functional app made for people to look up recipes.

My challenge is to create a responsive web app that is user friendly and can easily accommodate people's needs - whether they're vegan or meat lovers, have food allergies or on a diet, tight on budget or time, our recipe website can always provide a solution to them.

Another important factor is that there's an emphasis on inclusiveness towards beginners, students and young adults who just begin to explore the wonders of the culinary world. We're incorporating easy step by step photo guides along with instruction videos with time stamp features.
