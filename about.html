My Story

Hi, my name is Lilian Cheng. I’ve always wanted to learn about how human behaviors work, how we are motivated to make decisions, and this is what encouraged me to pursue a degree in psychology. Creativity is also something I can ’t live without, and the combination of both is why I decided to transition into UI/UX design.

I want to use what I studied, in conjunction with my passion for visual design to create better solutions and products for companies and users. Coming from a multicultural background also helps me tremendously when I try to empathize and think from the perspectives of diverse users.
